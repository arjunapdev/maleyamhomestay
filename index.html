About Kadalundi

     Kadalundi is one of the most beautiful ecotourism spots in Kerala. It is a small village in the southern part of Calicut. The firth where Arabian see meets Chaliyar river is one major attraction of this place.Kadalundi is very diverse in the scenario of birds, since it is one of the bird sanctuaries of kerala. It is home for various migrated birds in the season time [mainly summer].The rich blue and green mangroved estuary will always fascinate the tourists and will force them to come there again to enjoy the beauty of nature’s gift.

     Kadalundi is the first community reserve in kerala. . The reserve is situated around 15 hector area between the Bay of Kadalundi River and the Arabian Sea. The mangrove forest, Kadalundi has all the possibilities of Agro tourism, Nature tourism, village tourism, safari tourism, monsoon, health tourism, pilgrim tourism and eco tourism.
